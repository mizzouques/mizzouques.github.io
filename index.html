WHO WE ARE

Legacy Rooted in Brotherhood


The Epsilon Delta Chapter of Omega Psi Phi Fraternity, Inc. was chartered on April 19, 1972 at the University of Missouri, Columbia. For over five decades, our chapter has upheld the sacred principles of Manhood, Scholarship, Perseverance, and Uplift on Mizzou's campus and in the Columbia community.

We are men of distinction, leaders in the classroom, on campus, and in service to our community. Our brotherhood is built on the belief that friendship is essential to the soul, and our actions reflect that conviction every day.

8TH DISTRICT RECOGNITION

Highest NPHC GPA
Three Consecutive Semesters

The Epsilon Delta Chapter earned the highest GPA among all NPHC organizations at the University of Missouri for three consecutive semesters, closing Fall 2025 with a 3.50. Our men lead not only in brotherhood but in the classroom.

Brother Kyle Harris was recognized as the 8th District Scholar of the Year, a testament to the standard this chapter sets and expects.
